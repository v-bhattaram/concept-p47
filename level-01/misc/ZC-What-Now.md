### Trigger:Find Type-P Terminal
- Trigger Type -> ForcedQuest
- Order 3
- **Cinematic:** No
- **BGMusic:** Ambient — slight hope tone added to underscore
- **Dialogs:**
  - SURD: Find a Type-P Terminal. It can hold my active node and allow portable transfer.
  - Player: Where?
  - SURD: Three possible locations within the crash zone. I am transmitting coordinates.
  - Player: And if none of them are intact?
  - SURD: Then keep looking. The alternative is worse.
- **Objectives:**
  - Search 3 possible Type-P Terminal locations in the crash area
  - Recover food and water from Zegas containers during the search
  - Return to SURD if a Type-P terminal is found
- **GameplayNotes:**
  - Type-P locations are marked on the map. Player needs the bike to reach them efficiently — this will be resolved in Phase 3.
  - For now, player searches on foot near the crash zone.
  - Action group completes when player has inspected at least one Type-P location and returns to SURD terminal with a status report.
